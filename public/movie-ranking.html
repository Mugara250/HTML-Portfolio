My all-time favorite movies

The following are my all-time favorite movies:

Drunken Master

This is one of the earliest movies by Jackie Chan, a well-decorated Chinese actor. It expresses his acting humor which is one of his exquisite qualities as an actor.

Hard Target

This is probably one of the most watched movies by the American-Belgian born, Jean Claude Van Damme. Van Damme expresses his impeccable martial art skills. The movie also features Arnold Vosloo, a South African-American actor who is known for playing the role of the bad guy in most of the movies he has played.
